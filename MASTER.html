Living document

Game summary target: ~200 words (edit in place as canon firms up).

A Drop of Time is a third-person narrative action-adventure about Mother and Father—parents stranded on separate planes of reality after a catastrophic fracture in time. What was one world is now a stack of inconsistent rules: memory, matter, and agency leak between layers. The Temporals and those who serve them embody the cost of siphoning and control. Players switch perspectives, solve discovery-first puzzles, fight and flee in set-piece encounters, and learn a ritual vocabulary—fire, objects, silhouette language—that ties plot, puzzles, and endings together. Traversal and combat are character-grounded systems (stamina, hit feel, fracture charge, and later the Budokai chase, which must stay consistent with lore). Levels follow a four-beat zone rhythm within each act; section data exported from Unity markers lives beside level-design docs. This HTML tree under DOCS/ is the source of truth for story, systems, production status, open decisions, and sprint execution (_SPRINTS/).

Execution checklists: _SPRINTS/sprint-0.html (Sprint 1 wiring) · _SPRINTS/backlog.html (Sprints 2–6, sections, definition of done). Index: _SPRINTS.

What to load per task (LLM)

Routing

  • Story / dialogue: MASTER → STORY → CHARACTERS (add LORE page from STORY for world-rule checks).
  • Puzzle: MASTER → PUZZLES → Lore & world rules → relevant LEVEL DESIGN pages.
  • Combat feel: MASTER → COMBAT → CHARACTERS → TECHNICAL (mechanic unlock ladder).
  • Level / section: MASTER → LEVEL DESIGN (act-*-horizontal + four-beat) → STORY → TRAVERSAL.
  • Art / VFX: MASTER → ART & VISUAL → CHARACTERS → STORY section that motivates the beat.
  • Audio: MASTER → AUDIO → STORY / CHARACTERS as needed for context.
  • Engineering / tools: MASTER → TECHNICAL → SPRINTS → PRODUCTION (open decisions if touching scope).

Full context: Load MASTER only first. State the task; pull additional pages on demand—do not paste the entire DOCS tree into one prompt unless explicitly requested.

Open decisions: If the task touches an unresolved question (Warden execution, Section 4 puzzle, hologram retention, etc.), load open-decisions-log.html in the same session.
