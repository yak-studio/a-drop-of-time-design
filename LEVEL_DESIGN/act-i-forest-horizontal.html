MASTER / LEVEL DESIGN / Act I — Forest

Act I — The Forest · horizontal canvas

Left → right journey. Markers = beats, encounters, transitions. Distances imply real traversal. Pair with plot & acts and four-beat rhythm.

Core rules (structural): (1) Combat tone — heavy, not fun-first. (2) Escalation — each fight evolves; cut duplicates. (3) Narrative integrated in fights. (4) Act I systems only: Fracture Swap, rune stones, core combat, Teleattack (mid-act), temporal puzzles (late). No full teleport fantasy yet. (5) Warden — answer to a question player didn’t know they asked, not a boss room.

Enemy hierarchy this act

~1 Temporal in combat progression (+ Warden scenario). Most fights deputized temporals. Approx 15–20 deputized across five combats (tune in shipping).

Marker beats

◆ THE GRAVEYARD — start

Valley floor; cairn; lucky stone on cairn (ambiguous placement). Character select: first parent playable; other plane via TAB.

Mother: “We were supposed to save her up there.” Father places object at cairn base. Father: “Then we climb.”

◆ FIRST STEPS — movement tutorial

Lush forest; glacial river audio; mountain through canopy. Learn move; other parent exists only as TAB swap target. No combat. Indifferent beauty.

◆ THE MARRIAGE — before first blood non-negotiable

First TAB: hear other parent. Banter about walking pace — grocery-run energy, not mission talk. Player must feel long marriage before Warden names Uncle.

◆ RUNE STONE 1 — punch

One punch glyph; hit once; both planes clear (e.g. fallen tree).

◆ TRAVERSAL — forest descent

Downhill, stream, cold water audio; canopy tightens.

◆ RUNE STONE 2 — parent-specific swap

Two stones: Mother-only and Father-only (1–3 hits each). Must swap to clear both planes.

◆ COMBAT 1 — panic defence

2–3 deputized; defensive, clumsy, trying to survive. First kill ugly; no victory sting. Player feeling: “They don’t want to fight… but I have to.”

◇ BOTTLENECK — rock hop / low crawl

Muffled audio; emerge to overlook.

◆ RUNE STONE 3 — block, dodge, combo

Playtest-flexible sequence; intent: combat rhythm learned through world.

◆ TRAVERSAL — river approach

Ruins, louder river.

◆ COMBAT 2 — first pushback

3–4 deputized; light coordination, callouts (“Hold them off!”). Block/dodge required.

◇ BOTTLENECK — heavy door

Both planes push same door; gives together. Beyond: warm clearing — wrong-warm.

◆ TENSION + LOVE — clearing

Small talk: cold / how daughter hated cold / pretended not to / “She got that from you” / “She got everything good from you.” Warmth makes next fight worse.

◆ COMBAT 3 — close quarters + first Temporal

Tight ruin space; 2–3 deputized + 1 Temporal. Temporal calm-terrified. After Temporal dies: Teleattack unlocks — violent, disorienting first use.

◆ GHOST MEMORY — the kitchen

Extraction: domestic kitchen, child asks about mountains. Parent gives no performance; player carries it.

◇ BOTTLENECK — low crawl

Mud, ice melt; ridge exit; forest thinning.

◆ TEMPORAL PUZZLE 1 — plane-swap mechanism

Obstruction differs per plane; swap to see and cooperate. Design TBD — must feel planes resisting teamwork.

◆ TRAVERSAL — the climb

Scree, ice patches; river below.

◆ COMBAT 4 — moral pressure

Defender / Hider / Runner mix. Real-time choice what to do; no UI morality. Teleattack on runner vs defender should feel different.

◆ TEMPORAL PUZZLE 2 — pattern dials

Symbols lit/dark differ by plane; toggle Mother/Father to complete pattern. Complexity TBD.

◇ BOTTLENECK — ice shelf squeeze

Breath visible; still clearing — wrong stillness.

◆ COMBAT 5 — the hold line

4–6 deputized; coordinated delay — buying time for warning ahead. Longest/hardest Act I fight; Teleattack + stamina across parents required.

◆ THE DIVERGENCE

Mother: “We fix this.” Father: “We measure the cost.” Visual fork for 2D orthographic needs design pass; dialogue locked.

◆ WARDEN APPEARS → CONFESSION → CHOICE → OUTCOME

Clearing, ruins; Warden still, already broken. Restricted movement / camera — collision not duel.

Confession arc: math stalling → names Uncle → one-day-away sabotage → “I was right” about drain + destroyed cure. Revival not revealed.

Choice: Let her finish vs Stop her. Execute → memory extraction like Temporal. Spare → warning/knowledge then break.

Self-teleport unlock either path — plane collapse concentrates power (see lore).

◆ AFTERMATH → ACT CLOSE

Heavy movement; grey sky; planes visible, slight desync glitch; hum → silence → Interlude I.

Thesis beat: player skill at hurting people for reasons they believe in; Warden mirror — “So was I.”
