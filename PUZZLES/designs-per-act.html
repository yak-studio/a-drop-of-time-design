Puzzle designs per act

Fracture-native problems keyed to unlock ladder.

Act I

Rune stones — physical stones in terrain; glyphs for punch / block / dodge / short combos. Correct sequence lights rune; stone sinks on both planes; clears blockers. Variants: single-action tutorial; parent-specific paired stones (swap required); combination stones (playtest-flexible ordering).

Temporal puzzles (late Act I) — (1) Plane-swap obstruction: different geometry per plane; observe both, act in sequence. (2) Pattern dials / plates — solution not visible from one plane alone. Replace older “rune stone 5/6” notion.

See Act I horizontal beats for placement alongside combats.

Act II

Item teleport — send small objects across boundary (keys, weights, levers). First puzzle: missing lever on one plane, lever scrap on other.

Self-teleport — short blink within plane to reach mechanism (e.g. bridge crank). Taught after item teleport to keep verbs distinct.

Graduation — gates requiring both teleports + dual-plane coordination.

Layout: Act II horizontal beats.

Act III

Dual-teleport gauntlet — sequence of linked gates (item → self → simultaneous coordination). Hardest puzzle; tests Act II teaching.

No new puzzle verbs — only pressure and combinatory depth.

Layout: Act III horizontal beats.
