Endings

Three unbearable outcomes. Exit order + siphoning rules v4.4. Pair with lore — Convergence.

Framing: No "good" ending — only which tragedy you can live with. The game does not moralise the menu. The gate and who walks first decide everything.

Rules recap

  • Walking into the Core from your plane survives you and collapses that plane.
  • After the daughter exits to the Core, the gate seals for parents — her presence collapses remaining parent planes (13th as event).
  • Order of exits determines A / B / C shape.
  • Uncle may offer his plane — shifts consequences, not a redemption arc. Working idea; see open decisions.

Ending A — Let her rest locked

Both parents walk out into the Core. Both planes collapse. They survive; they can touch each other for the first time since the prologue.

The daughter stays on her plane — contained. Siphoning is resolved; the world stabilises. She had already accepted staying; the parents almost undid it. They never know she chose. Companion remains with her.

Image: stillness; shimmer of her; lucky stone on summit stone — ambiguity echo of the cairn.

The fourth ritual follows — see the ritual. The only time the parents perform it together. The only time they can.

Final sting: nickname TBD. Rarity rule applies — see four-beat / nickname rule.

Ending B — The sacrifice locked

One parent walks through. Their plane collapses; they are gone.

Daughter remains contained. The other parent remains on their plane. One plane collapse is not enough to rebalance — siphoning continues; the world is still dying slowly. The surviving parent gained nothing but loss.

Cruel because it is pointless.

Ending C — The absolute orphan locked

Daughter walks first. Both parent planes collapse; the parents become absence. She wakes in the Core — alive, alone; world unified; no siphoning.

Uncle variable: kneels / drained / absent — gear in snow. Lucky stone; "That counts." Companion on the empty daughter plane. Long hold on final image — player stops when ready.

Hidden cost variant

An exit order where the daughter lives alongside a surviving parent in the Core implies an expiration-date world — she is the 13th draw alive among them, siphoning slowly resuming. Document as discoverable consequence, not a main-menu ending label.
